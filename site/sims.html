Why Sims, not synthetic users

Most AI personas make people up. Ours are made of real ones.

Made-up users with a made-up demographic tell you nothing. Klavity Sims are built from the voice of your actual customers, and they react to the product you actually shipped.

⛓ from real calls

Grounded in verbatim quotes

Every pain, want and love links back to the exact line a customer said. No invented opinions — you can trace each insight to its source.

👁 reads your screen

Reacts to your actual product

Sims walk your live pages and respond to what they see — not to a written spec. The feedback is about the thing you actually built.

🌱 evolving

Personas evolve with every call

Add another interview and your Sims sharpen — new traits, new quotes, a richer picture of who you're really building for.

🎫 filed

Files grounded bugs to your tracker

A reaction isn't a dead-end note. It becomes a ready-to-fix ticket in Plane, Jira, GitHub or Linear — with the persona and quote attached, duplicates collapsed.
