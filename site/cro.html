    Frequently asked questions

    What does the CRO Vibe Check analyse?

    It fetches your page's public HTML and uses an AI model to identify friction points that reduce conversion — unclear CTAs, missing social proof, confusing copy, flow friction, and more. It surfaces up to 8 issues ranked by severity.

    Is my page content stored?

    No. The page text is fetched once, passed to the AI for analysis, and discarded. We only store your email (if you unlock) and the funnel event so we can follow up. See our privacy policy.

    What's the difference between this and Klavity?

    This tool gives you a one-off snapshot. Klavity runs your Sims — AI personas based on your real customers — continuously against every page, surfacing regressions and recurring friction the moment they appear.

    Does it work on password-protected or SPAs?

    It analyses the publicly-served HTML. For password-protected pages or heavy SPAs that render client-side, the result may be partial. Klavity's AutoSims drive a real browser and can log in.
