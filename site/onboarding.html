▦Welcome to Klavity

Your customers review your product — before they ever see it.

Klavity turns your real customer calls into Sims — AI personas grounded in the exact words your customers said. They walk your product and react in character, so you catch what they'd hate before they leave — without booking a single call. It also includes Snap — a one-click bug reporter your site visitors can use right now, no calls needed.

"It's like having your hardest customers in the room as you build."
