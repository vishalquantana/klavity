▦Welcome to Klavity

Your customers review your product — before they ever see it.

Klavity turns your real customer calls into Sims — AI personas grounded in the exact words your customers said. They walk your product and react in character, so you catch what they'd hate before they churn — without booking a single call.

01
🎙

Bring your customers in

Drop in a sales or support call. Each speaker becomes a Sim — their pains, wants, and the quotes behind them. No call handy? Start with a ready-made review team.

02
👀

They watch your real product

Connect your product's URL. The moment you — or a teammate — open it, your Sims pop up and react in-character, live on the page. Usability testing that runs itself.

03

Turn reactions into tickets

Every reaction is one click from a bug or feature ticket — screenshot, page, and the customer's actual quote attached. The "why" travels with the work.
