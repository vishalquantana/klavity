The service

Klavity is an AI-assisted quality tool. The browser extension (Klavity Snap) and cloud backend (Klavity Cloud) let AI personas — called Sims — review web pages you consent to and automatically file bug and feature tickets to the issue tracker you connect. Klavity is currently in early access and is provided as-is. Features may change, and we may need to take the service down occasionally for maintenance.
