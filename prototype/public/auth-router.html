Google, Okta, or other SSO

AutoSim uses a pre-authenticated session for your app. The IdP redirect and MFA flow are skipped — everything on the other side of sign-in is still fully explored.

What this means: When your app is SSO-only, AutoSim will use a pre-authenticated session for the walk. The login UX itself (IdP redirect, MFA, etc.) will not be simmed — but every page after sign-in will be, including any post-login onboarding or consent screens.

To enable this, contact Klavity support or set up a Test Account in Settings with a pre-authenticated session token.
