Sims (auto-review)

Let Sims automatically review pages
On = Sims review monitored pages and post feedback. Off = no automatic reviews anywhere.
Privacy: When Sims review a page, Klavity captures a screenshot of the visible area plus the page's structure and sends it to Klavity to generate feedback. This only happens on pages your team is monitoring, and only after you've given consent the first time. Turn the toggle off to stop all automatic reviews. You can also pause Sims on any page using the "Sims reviewing" pill in the bottom-right corner.
